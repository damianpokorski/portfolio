What is MyHealthUK

My Health UK is an interactive platform for primary care to engage with patients.
The goal of this platform is to provide an extra layer of communication between patients, nurses, and their doctors.
Patient side of the platform consisting of a mobile app, which allowed patients to receive messages, submit their vitals, request as well as accept appointments. Staff side of the platform provided a web interface that allowed the clinicians to interact with patients, in a way never done before. Clinicians had access to patient details, could monitor their patient's vitals, send and book appointments, and also build a library of information to be accessible to the patients.
